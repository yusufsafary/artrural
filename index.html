The Mission

Three Pillars of Rural Web3

artrural.fun is built on the belief that village culture is not a relic — it's a living, breathing asset that deserves a permanent place on the blockchain.

01
Art from the Soil
Every NFT carries full provenance — the artisan's name, their village, their cultural lineage. Verified on-chain, forever.
02
Village DAO Governance
Villages become sovereign economic units. Community members hold governance tokens to vote on local projects and resource allocation.
03
Living Cultural Archive
Oral histories, sacred songs, and ancestral recipes minted as cultural NFTs — not for sale, but preserved collectively for future generations.

Process

From Village Hand
to Global Wallet

1
🌾
Village Onboarding
Local coordinators visit artisans, document their craft, heritage, and story. Every creator gets a self-sovereign digital identity.
2
📸
Cultural Capture
Works are photographed, digitized, and contextualised with cultural metadata — lineage, technique, materials, regional lore.
3
⛓️
On-Chain Minting
Each work is minted as an NFT with immutable provenance. The artisan owns their token contract and receives royalties automatically.
4
🌍
Global Marketplace
Collectors worldwide discover, bid, and acquire. 80% of every sale goes directly to the artisan. 10% funds the Village DAO treasury.

Governance

The Village DAO

Each participating village forms its own DAO. Community members hold governance tokens used to decide on fund allocation, new artisan onboarding, and local infrastructure projects. Outsiders can stake tokens to co-invest in a village's economic future and earn yield from cultural royalties.
